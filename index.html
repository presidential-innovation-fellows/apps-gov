How are products selected?

The goal of Apps.Gov is to facilitate an active marketplace to support government buyers when procuring cloud technologies. Join the community to learn more and get help with selling to the federal government at http://discuss.apps.gov.

Information on the product, company, existing contract vehicles, and government approvals is listed on the products page. Companies are encouraged to submit or update their product information via the open source GitHub repository for accuracy and competitive advantage.

If you have any questions regarding Apps.Gov or information listed, please reach out to apps-gov@gsa.gov
